Our Mission Statement

Mission
SEARCH Homeless Services pursues a mission of providing hope, creating opportunity, and transforming lives. SEARCH would like your creative ideas on how we can leverage technology to attract and manage a volunteer workforce that can operate in support of our mission. We know that there are a lot of tools out in the market, but most of them focus on the operational side of volunteerism and management of volunteers. We are looking to invert this paradigm and want our volunteers to become "ambassadors" of the SEARCH mission. We want to empower the self service functions and encourage them to engage their social network to really accelerate SEARCH's reach and social impact.

Core Values
SEARCH Homeless Services is committed to providing quality services to our program participants, and being good neighbors to our community and responsible stewards of our resources. SEARCH’s distinctive character is evident in our interactions with clients, the community and with each other, and our core values are the foundation of our culture. We base our high standards of service on five basic principles. Every decision we make—from client matters to strategic positioning—is tested and measured against these principles.

Our Mission Statement

Integrity
We do the right thing and make the right choice.

Trust
We are credible, competent and keep our commitments.

Authentic Communication
We respectfully exchange ideas, information and perspectives.

Client-Centered
We make clients the central focus of our work.

Learning Organization
We foster education, innovation and discovery.
